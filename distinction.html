Crisis of Distinction

2019
Wood, Steel, Digital Print

The work consists of several mounted texts and digital still images distributed through the space generated through photogrammetric modeling. The still images are placed so as to produce a sequence of ambulatory visual interpolation of a film which does not exist, where each image and text are optically located in relation so as to produce a narrative sequence.
